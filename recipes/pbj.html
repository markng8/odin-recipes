Grilled Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

Learn how to make a grilled peanut butter and jelly sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspons butter
  • 2 slices white bread
  • 2 teaspoons any flavor fruit jelly
  • 1 teaspoon peanut butter

Directions

  1. Step 1 Gather ingrdients
  2. Step 2 Heat skillet to 350 degrees
  3. Step 3 Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread. Spread jelly on the unbuttered side of one slice of bread; spread peanut butter on the other unbuttered side.
  4. Step 4 Place bread slices together, so peanut butter and jelly are in the middle.
  5. Step 5 Cook on the preheated skillet until golden brown and heated through, about 4 minutes per side. Serve and enjoy!
