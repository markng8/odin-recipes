Grilled Cheese

Description

Learn how to make a grilled cheese sandwich.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white breads
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Directions

  1. Step 1 Gather ingrdients
  2. Step 2 Preheat skillet over medium heat. Butter bread
  3. Step 3 Please bread butter side down on hot skillet and add 1 slice of cheese
  4. Step 4 Butter second slice and place butter side up on top of cheese
  5. Step 5 Cook until light browned on one side;flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted
  6. Step 6 Repeat the remaining 2 slices of bread, butter and slice of cheese. Serve and enjoy!
